RFID BASED SCANNER TO FIND LOST THINGS

By using Arduino UNO and RFID tags, designed a scanner to find the lost personal things like watch , wallet , mobile phones . RFID will recognize the things within some specific range of distance.

BMS USING DIGITAL TWIN TECHNOLOGY

A battery management system is designed using the digital twin technology. Battery parameters like voltage, current and temperature were taken from real time battery model and analysed by using kalman filter and predicted the SOC and SOH. Finally the battery parameters were interfaced and twinned by using digital twin technology.
